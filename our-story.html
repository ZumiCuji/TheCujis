How We Met

We first crossed paths at a frat party during our freshman year of college. I was so taken with Morgan that I claimed she was my girlfriend before I had even spoken to her. She quickly humbled me by refusing to give me her number, but somehow, we soon became inseparable. She made that first year of college unforgettable, and to this day, she continues to make my life just as amazing. - Zumi

The Proposal

I wanted the proposal to symbolize our future. Morgan and I want a small farm near the beach. So, I chose a small farm near the beach to be the place where I asked the love of my life to be my forever person. - Zumi
